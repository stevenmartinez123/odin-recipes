Lasagna

The tradition of lasagna originated in Emilia Romagna, and from there it has become one of the symbols of Italian cuisine throughout the world. Abroad, lasagne alla bolognese, made with b√©chamel and meat sauce, is well known. The Neapolitan version of this recipe includes among its ingredients tomato sauce, mozzarella, meatballs and Roman ricotta cheese. In Liguria, pesto is used as a sauce, while in Venice the sauce is replaced with red radicchio. The lasagna you eat depends on where you are.

Ingredients

  • 1 pack of lasagna noodles
  • 1/2 pound of mozerella cheese
  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 1 can of prego tomato sauce
  • 1 can of vegetable juice
  • 1 onion
  • 2 tablespoons of olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons of oregano
  • salt
  • Pepper
  • Pinch of Garlic Seasoning
  • 1 tablespoon of Italian Seasoning
  • Pinch of Cayenne Pepper

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 degreen farenheight
  2. Pour water in a large bowl and heat to boiling
  3. Place 2 tablespoons of oil in a pan set to medium heat
  4. Cut and dice onion and place in pan
  5. when sizling place beef into pan and cook until brown
  6. Season meat with salt, pepper, garlic seasoning, italian seasoning, and cayenne pepper
  7. Once water is boiling place lasagna noodles in pan and cook until soft
  8. Once meat is cooked pour tomato sauce and vegetable juice in same pan
  9. Season sauce with salt, pepper, garlic seasoning, and oregano until slightly boiling
  10. Place cooked noodles evenly in a glass pan until pan is covered
  11. Cover layer of noodles with meat sauce and cover with cheese
  12. Repeat until out of noodles or sauce
  13. Place in preheated oven for 20-30 minutes until cheese if baked on top
  14. Let cool for 10 minutes then enjoy!!
